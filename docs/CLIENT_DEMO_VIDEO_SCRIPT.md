## 🔊 VOICEOVER RECORDING NOTES

---

### Recording Setup:
- **Microphone**: Professional USB or XLR mic
- **Environment**: Quiet room with sound dampening
- **Distance**: 6-8 inches from mic
- **Posture**: Standing or sitting upright
- **Hydration**: Water nearby, no dairy before recording

### Delivery Style:
- **Pace**: 140-160 words per minute (moderate)
- **Tone**: Conversational yet professional
- **Energy**: Medium-high (enthusiastic but not manic)
- **Emphasis**: Stress key words and numbers
- **Pauses**: Natural breaks between sections

### Technical Settings:
- **Sample Rate**: 48kHz
- **Bit Depth**: 24-bit
- **Format**: WAV (uncompressed) for editing
- **Export**: MP3 320kbps or AAC for final
- **Noise Floor**: -60dB or lower

### Post-Processing:
1. Remove breath sounds (selectively)
2. EQ for clarity (reduce low-end rumble, boost presence around 3-5kHz)
3. Compression (3:1 ratio, gentle)
4. De-esser (reduce harsh S sounds)
5. Normalization to -3dB peak
6. Final limiter for safety
